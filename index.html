BilliardsTracker

BilliardsTracker is a tool which helps you to log Pool, Snooker, Carom training exercise results.

Add your favourite drills and start tracking successful or missed shots using your Apple Watch. Intuitive gestures allow you to take your mind away from counting balls and concentrate on what is the most important - making shots. You can immediately see current session statistics or later view entire drill history and progress chart to see your improvement over time.

Take your billiards game to the next level!
